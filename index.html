About PODVEST

PODVEST is a Decentralised Platforrm with the ability to engage in multiple transactions. This Platform allocates funds to Participants who activates their accounts after a certain referrals they have made.

Direct-To-Consumer Decentralize Platform

This basically is peer-to-peer system network , which remotely connects to each other to complete a function or a task.

In peer-to-peer network, Participants both provide and use resources. Using peer-to-peer networking will involve making transactions from one user to another without the need of an intermediary

peer-to-peer networks often contains large number of users who utilizes resources shared by other nodes.
